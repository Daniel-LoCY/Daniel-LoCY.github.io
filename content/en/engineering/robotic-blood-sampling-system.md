This project combines depth sensing, computer vision, and robotic control to support blood sampling tasks with a collaborative robotic arm.

## Highlights

- Identified vessel locations with Azure Kinect and OpenCV.
- Detected tools with YOLOv8 for task awareness.
- Integrated perception and robot control into a single workflow.
